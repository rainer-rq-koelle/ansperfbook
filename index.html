Preface

Operational performance analysis of air transportation and air navigation is essential to address the capabilities of local or regional systems, and the impact of capabilities or operational procedures.

Operational performance monitoring and reporting has become a higher visibility through the recent Global Air Navigation Plan (GANP). Through the GANP, International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO) links future ambitions with performance and stresses the need for local and regional performance monitoring. Operational performance monitoring is well established in the US and Europe. In the US, the Federal Aviation Authority (FAA), and in Europe, the Performance Review Unit (PRU), publish regular regional performance monitoring reports. Both groups work together on a set of harmonised measures to compare the operational performance of the US and European air navigation system.

Expanding the scope of performance reporting and enable other parties to validate the results requires harmonised interpretation and implementation of the respective metrics.

To support benchmarking comparisons amongst different parties, the aim of this project is to develop an open-sourced based data analytical package ensuring the joint and harmonised application and implementation of performance metrics.

This book is an attempt of making use of R/RStudio and supporting packages (e.g. readr, dplyr) to harmonise the application of operational performance indicators. As part of the R/RStudio universe, this book has been produced with the bookdown package.

The documentation is provided in the hope that it is useful to the reader. Please report back about problems or queries you have relating to the use of the material presented or the corresponding R package.
